Mental Health & Wellness

This page connects people with immediate support, treatment locators, peer resources, and practical next steps. It does not replace licensed medical or mental-health care.

Need help right now?

Call or text 988 to reach the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. If there is immediate danger, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department.

Grief and Loss

Grief can affect sleep, concentration, energy, relationships, and physical health. Support from trusted people, peer groups, faith communities, or licensed professionals can help.

Stress, Burnout, and Isolation

Start small: eat, hydrate, sleep when possible, reduce immediate demands, contact one trusted person, and make one appointment or support call. Persistent or worsening symptoms deserve professional attention.

  • NIMH: Find help for mental illness
  • Healthy ways to cope

Helping Someone Else

Listen without arguing, take threats seriously, reduce access to immediate danger when safe, and connect the person with 988 or emergency care.
