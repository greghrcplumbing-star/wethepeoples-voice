Core Team Standards

Truth Before Speed

Do not claim a connection, deployment, worker, result, endorsement, or partnership until it has been directly verified.

People Before Ego

Listen first, explain plainly, protect dignity, and focus on practical help rather than credit.

Privacy by Default

Publish only public contact information. Keep private phone numbers, family records, credentials, and administrative data off the public site.

Sources and Dates

Use current primary sources for legal, medical, financial, government, and public-benefit information.

Visible Accountability

Document major decisions, identify unfinished work, disclose corrections, and maintain recoverable version history.

Accessibility

Build for mobile devices, keyboard navigation, readable contrast, clear headings, descriptive links, and image alt text.
